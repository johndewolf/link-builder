Social Link Generator

As a marketer you want people to share your content, right? Right. You can use the tool above to generate links for your content that will allow users to share it to their social networks with a single click. Currently it creates links for Google Plus, Facebook, Twitter (with an option to add in custom Tweet text), and LinkedIn (with the option to add in a custom Title and Summary). All you have to do is fill in the URL and optional fields, copy the generated URLs and hyperlink them on your page.

Suggested uses include: Blogs, Resource Landing Pages, and News Articles.
