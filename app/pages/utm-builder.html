UTM Builder

Do you track campaigns in Google Analytics, Marketo, HubSpot or other analytics software? Have you been forced to painstakingly add each, individual parameter to the end of the URLs? Have you ruined your fingers with arthritis just because you wanted to track source, campaign, and medium in your web analytics software? Well suffer no more!

Use the tool above automatically append values for the source, campaign, and mediums. Suggested uses included tracking conversion rates for Pay Per Click (PPC) Campaigns, Newsletters, and Social Media.

Term Definitions:

  • Source - (utm_source) - This parameter is used to identify the specific property the traffic came from
  • Medium - (utm_medium) – Used to identify the category of the source from where the traffic came from.
  • Campaign - (utm_campaign) – Used to identify the name of the of campaign that the link is associated with.

Examples:

Source Medium Campaign
twitter social-media holiday-sale
newsletter email holiday-sale
ppc search holiday-sale
